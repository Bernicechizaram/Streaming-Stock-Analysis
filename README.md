Streaming Wars: Netflix vs Disney Market Analysis (2020 to 2024)
Project Overview

This project analyzes the stock performance of Netflix and Disney over a four year period. The goal was to compare growth, volatility, and market behavior using clean, structured data and an interactive dashboard.

You will see how each company responded to the 2022 market downturn and how they performed during the 2024 recovery.

Business Problem

Comparing competing companies is difficult when:

data comes from different timelines

price and volume are not aligned

raw datasets lack clear performance metrics

This project solves that by creating a clean dataset and a dashboard that allows direct comparison of stock price growth and trading volume.

What I Built

Cleaned and aligned 5 plus years of stock data using Python

Engineered financial metrics such as cumulative return and daily return

Built an interactive Tableau dashboard with KPI tracking

Designed dual axis charts to compare price movement and trading volume

Implemented global filters for real time company level analysis

Key Insights

Netflix delivered higher overall returns but showed higher volatility

Disney maintained more stable performance due to diversified revenue streams

Netflix experienced a sharp decline during the 2022 subscriber loss period

Netflix showed stronger recovery and outperformed Disney in 2024

Stock price movements for Netflix reacted more strongly to market events
